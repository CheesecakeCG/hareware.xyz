Shorkobot is a social VR avatar originally made for the 2024 VRChat Summer Jam, with the theme "Summer Music Festival".

  • Good Performance Ranking on PC & Medium on Mobile
  • AudioLink Integration, including a "Holo-Ocean" effect
  • LTCGI & VRCLV Support for better lighting in clubs & movie worlds!
  • Don't like the default look? There's In-game Customization:
    • Hue-Shift Sliders with Quest Support!
    • Texture Layer Toggles (for those that prefer not to be a bot, or don't want to be covered in stickers.)
    • Tail Puppet!
    • 6 Body Shape Sliders that go bigger and smaller!
  • Full-Body Tracking Support
  • Quest & Android Phone Support
  • Uses Unity Prefab Variants for easier editing

While the avatar itself is mostly complete, this project as whole is still a work-in-progress! Blender files as well as VRM, Resonite, and Overte support are planned and there is still further room for optimization.
